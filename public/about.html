THE KING'S CUP

What is The King's Cup?

The King's Cup is a card based drinking game where each player takes a turn at drawing a card and doing the rule of that card.

What do you need for The King's Cup?

All you need to play The King's Cup is a pack of classic playing cards and some friends who are willing to get blackout drunk.
