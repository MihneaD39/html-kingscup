RULES OF THE KING'S CUP

ACE-WATERFALL

The player that draws the card controls the pace.

When he starts drinking, you start drinking.

You may only stop when he stops.

TWO-YOU

You choose someone to drink.

THREE-ME

You drink.

FOUR-WAITER

The player that draws the card is the waiter 'till another 4 is drawn.

The waiter has to keep the glasses full.

FIVE-GUYS

The guys drink.

SIX-CHICKS

The girls drink.

SEVEN-HEAVEN

Every player has to reach one hand up to the heaven.

The last player to do so has to drink.

EIGHT-MATE

The player that draws this card can choose another player to be his mate.

If you're the mate of a player, when you drink, he/she drinks.

PRO TIP:Make sure to always choose a player that already has a mate so that when you drink they'll also drink!

NINE-RHYME

The player that draws this card has to say a word.

The other players try to say a word that rhymes.

The player that fails to do so, has to drink.

TEN-CATEGORIES

The player that draws this card has to choose a category.

The other players try to say the right words that fit into that category.

The player that fails to do so, has to drink.

JACK-RULES

The player that draws this card has to choose a rule for the entire game.

All the players must follow the rules for the rest of the game.

The player that fails to do so, has to drink.

QUEEN-QUESTIONS

The player that draws this card becomes THE QUEEN OF QUESTIONS

Any player who answers to THE QUEEN OF QUESTIONS has to drink

KING-KING'S CUP

HAIL TO THE KING!

Every player has to drink.
